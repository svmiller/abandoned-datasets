      There are three other files accompanying this introductory text:

      1) kant699a.dta are the data used in John R. Oneal and Bruce Russett,
"Assessing the Kantian Peace: The Pacific Benefits of Democracy, Interdependence,
and International Organizations, 1885-1992," World Politics, 52 (October 1999), pp.
1-37.  The data are in Stata 5.0 format.

      2) wptable1.log is a Stata output file, in ascii, that contains the analyses
reported in Table 1 of the article.

      3) wptable3.log is a Stata output file, in ascii, that contains the analyses
reported in Table 3 of the article.
